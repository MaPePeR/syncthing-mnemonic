Syncthing Mnemonic Converter

Convert a Syncthing DeviceID to and from 24 english words, which are easier to type, read and dictate.
Uses a wordlist from the trezor/python-mnemonic project.

Information: At the moment the code is limited to only generate the old format of the Syncthing DeviceIDs. Don't be surprised, when the resulting DeviceID looks a little bit different.
It will also not use checksum that is integrated in the new DeviceID format.
